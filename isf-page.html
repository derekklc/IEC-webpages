EXHIBITION

IEC Promotion Center Exhibition Hall covers an area of 950 m². The theme of the exhibition hall center around IEC's remarkable development throughout modern civilization, the journey of electro-technical standards, as well as China's participation and contribution to international standardization progress. With modern technological design and interactive presentation, the exhibition tour shows our guests how IEC standards benefits multiple aspects of our daily life, which embodies the IEC's vision of "IEC everywhere for a safer and more efficient world".
